Uganda's delegation attended the IMF/World Bank Spring Meetings 2026 in Washington, D.C., engaging with international partners on development policy and financing.

Minister Hon. Henry Musasizi represented Uganda in discussions on the Development Policy Operation (DPO) and Extended Credit Facility arrangements with the IMF.

Key discussion points included:
- Uganda's macroeconomic performance and growth outlook
- Progress on structural reforms
- Next steps for budget support financing
- Coordination with World Bank on infrastructure projects

The meetings reinforced Uganda's strong partnership with multilateral development institutions and secured continued support for the country's National Development Plan.
