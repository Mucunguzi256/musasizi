Hon. Henry Musasizi, Minister of State for Finance (General Duties), led Uganda's delegation to the US-Uganda Business and Investment Forum in Washington, D.C., where he appealed to American investors to participate in Uganda's ambitious $500 billion GDP target by 2040.

The Minister highlighted Uganda's strong GDP growth trajectory and the numerous investment opportunities across sectors including agriculture, energy, infrastructure, and manufacturing.

Key points from the Minister's presentation:
- Uganda's GDP growth averaging 6% annually over the past decade
- Investment incentives including tax holidays and strategic location
- Growing middle class consumer base of over 45 million people
- Abundant natural resources including oil, minerals, and agricultural land

The forum brought together over 200 American business leaders and potential investors, with discussions on joint ventures in sectors critical to Uganda's development.

Musasizi emphasized Uganda's commitment to creating a favorable business environment and transparent regulatory framework to attract foreign direct investment.
