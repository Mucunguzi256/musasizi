Hon. Henry Musasizi, Minister of State for Finance (General Duties), addressed global finance ministers at the International Conference on Climate Finance, highlighting the significant fiscal impact of climate change on national budgets.

In his address, the Minister emphasized that climate change is no longer just an environmental issue but a fundamental challenge to fiscal sustainability and economic planning.

Key points from the Minister's address:
- Uganda faces increased flooding and drought events affecting agricultural output
- Climate adaptation costs are diverting resources from development priorities
- Need for international climate finance mechanisms to support developing nations
- Call for debt-for-climate swap mechanisms

Musasizi called for enhanced international cooperation and increased climate finance flows to help developing countries like Uganda meet adaptation and mitigation costs without compromising economic development.

The conference concluded with a joint statement committing to integrate climate considerations into fiscal policy frameworks.
